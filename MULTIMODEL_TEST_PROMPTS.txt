# MULTIMODEL_TEST_PROMPTS

Prompt 1 (Reasoning):

If a meeting is scheduled for two hours after 3:15 PM, but is delayed by 45 minutes, what time will the meeting start, expressed in HH:MM PM format in a single sentence?

Prompt 2 (Creativity):

Write a single-sentence horror story of exactly ten words about a mirror.

Prompt 3 (Math):

In one sentence, provide the result of multiplying the number of sides on a hexagon by the number of planets in our solar system.
